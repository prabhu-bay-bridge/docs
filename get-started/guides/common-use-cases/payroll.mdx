---
title: "Payroll"
---

Enable employers to pay employees and contractors globally by **creating a wallet and virtual account **for the employer and a wallet for each employee. Fiat deposits into the virtual account are converted to stablecoins and held in the employer’s wallet. Use the Transfers API to pay out funds from the employer to the employee.
