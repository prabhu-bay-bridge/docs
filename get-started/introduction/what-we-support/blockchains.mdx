## Note

- ⚠️ **Deposits sent to unsupported asset/blockchain pairs** or to **mismatched addresses** may be **irretrievable and permanently lost**. Please review our list of supported blockchains carefully before initiating a payment.
- The following stablecoins and blockchains are supported across all Bridge APIs.
- **USDT is not supported for users in the EEA** due to regulatory restrictions.
- **USDB** is Bridge’s native stablecoin and **earns rewards automatically** when held.
- Interested in support for other assets or blockchains? **Contact us directly** to discuss integration.
