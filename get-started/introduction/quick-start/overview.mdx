## üîÅ Orchestration: Move money

Bridge orchestration APIs power your fiat and crypto flows. Go from fiat to crypto (onramps), crypto to fiat (offramps), and crypto to crypto.

| Product                       | Description                                                                                        | Use when you need to...                                                          |
| :---------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Virtual Accounts**          | **Unique fiat deposit addresses** (ACH, Wire, SEPA, SPEI) issued to your customers.                | Collect fiat bank transfers - especially for recurring deposits.                 |
| **Transfers**                 | **One-time fiat or stablecoin transfers**. Supports onramps, offramps, and crypto-to-crypto flows. | When you need to send or receive money programmatically - either fiat or crypto. |
| **Static Template Transfers** | **Reusable transfer instructions** for **recurring**, template-based transfers.                    | Let customers make recurring payments.                                           |
| **Liquidation Addresses**     | **On-chain addresses** that auto-forward to a crypto or fiat destination.                          | Accept crypto at fixed addresses and automatically route or convert funds.       |
| **Prefunded Accounts**        | Load fiat into Bridge-managed balances for instant offramps.                                       | **Guarantee fast withdrawals** without waiting for inbound deposits to settle.   |
