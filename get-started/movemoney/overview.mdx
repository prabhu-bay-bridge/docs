Bridge APIs are lego blocks that let you build delightful payment experiences for your customers. The guides in this section will walk you through the following APIs.

| **API**                       | **Description**                                                                                                       | **When to Use**                                                                                          |
| :---------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Virtual Accounts**          | **Unique fiat deposit addresses** issued to your customers so they can receive fiat deposits (ACH, Wire, SEPA, SPEI). | When you want to collect fiat payments for your customers via bank transfers, especially for repeat use. |
| **Transfers**                 | **One-time fiat or stablecoin transfers**. Supports onramps, offramps, and crypto-to-crypto flows.                    | When you need to send or receive money programmatically - either fiat or crypto.                         |
| **Static Template Transfers** | **Reusable transfer instructions** for **recurring**, template-based transfers.                                       | When your customer want to accept recurring payments.                                                    |
| **Liquidation Address**       | Permanent on-chain address that **auto-forwards incoming crypto to a fia**t or crypto destination.                    | When you need a **fixed blockchain address** and want funds automatically converted or routed.           |
