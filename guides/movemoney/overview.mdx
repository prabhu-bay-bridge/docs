title: "Orchestration"
---

Bridge APIs are lego blocks that let you build delightful payment experiences for your customers. The guides in this section will walk you through the following APIs.

| **API**                                                                           | **Description**                                                                                                       |
| --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [**Virtual Accounts**](/orchestration/virtual_account/virtual_account)            | **Unique fiat deposit addresses** issued to your customers so they can receive fiat deposits (ACH, Wire, SEPA, SPEI). |
| [**Transfers**](/orchestration/transfer/transfer)                                 | One-time **fiat or stablecoin** transfers. Supports onramps, offramps, and crypto-to-crypto flows.                    |
| [**Static Template Transfers**](/orchestration/transfer/statictemplates)          | Take **recurring transfers** with reusable transfer instructions.                                                     |
| [**Liquidation Address**](/orchestration/liquidation_address/liquidation_address) | **Permanent on-chain address** that auto-forwards incoming crypto to a fiat or crypto destination.                    |
