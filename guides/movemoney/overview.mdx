title: "Orchestration"
---

Bridge APIs are lego blocks to build delightful payment experiences for your customers. The guides in this section will walk you through the following APIs.

| **Product**                   | **Description**                                                                                                       |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Transfers**                 | One-time **fiat or stablecoin** transfers. Supports onramps, offramps, and crypto-to-crypto flows.                    |
| **Static Template Transfers** | Take **recurring transfers** with reusable transfer instructions.                                                     |
| **Virtual Accounts**          | **Unique fiat deposit addresses** issued to your customers so they can receive fiat deposits (ACH, Wire, SEPA, SPEI). |
| **Liquidation Address**       | **Permanent on-chain address** that auto-forwards incoming crypto to a fiat or crypto destination.                    |
