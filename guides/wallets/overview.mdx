---
title: "Custodial Wallets"
---

Bridge offers fully managed, compliant custody for stablecoins with no third-party integrations required.

Using Bridge wallets, you can:

- Create wallets for your customers or your business.
- Transfer stablecoins in and out using orchestration APIs.
- Query balances and transfer history.
- Tag and organize wallets as needed.

Before creating wallets, make sure you have onboarded your customer and finished any required KYC/KYB or endorsements.

Refer the [Wallets](doc:custodial-wallets) section for supported chains and currencies, restrictions, and more in depth information. Refer the [Bridge Wallets](ref:bridge-wallets) API reference to see all the wallet APIs.
