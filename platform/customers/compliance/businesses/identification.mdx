---
title: "Business Identification Numbers by Region"
description: "The following lists identification numbers that Bridge will accept for businesses by region."
mode: "wide"
---

In addition to the items listed in the table below:

- `other` are acceptable tax identification types for all countries.
- `ssn`, `itin`, `ein` and `passport` are are acceptable for US based sole-proprietorships.
- `passport` and `national_id` are acceptable for non-US-based sole-proprietorships.
